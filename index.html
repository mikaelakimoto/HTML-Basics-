Tom Nook from Nintendo's Animal Crossing



Tom Nook is a vital character in Nintendo's game series Animal Crossing

Tom Nook is your landlord in the game, and in original games, he checks in on you frequently to make sure you are okay. He, along with other characters in the game hold hints to inter-personal relationships with the other "villagers" and how to best enjoy the game.
